Emerging from a Pandemic: Are we going back to business as usual?

How do we chart a path to get out of the pandemic for businesses and all?
In light of the COVID-19 pandemic, many people and businesses lost their source of livelihood, as the idea of doing business and work completely changed. As the world went into a shutdown, to control the pandemic, a lot of these businesses totally shutdown, rendering many dependent on those, without jobs and a source of stable income.

The scope of this studio is to study this way of business, why and how it failed, and how it could be revived to fit in the 'new normal'.




How would the world be if we reimagined and reinterpreted the historically taken-for-granted materials, communications, theories, practices and processes?

Behavioral Design and its impace on the way products/services are percieved.

People buy things that they find attractive, either visually or through some special features. To promote a new product into the market, most businesses use creative ways to popularize them, for eg. a newely launched burger at McDonalds will be placed centrally on the menu with a picture so that customers notice it first. The businesses target certain 'behaviours' of their customers and provide visual triggers for them to buy it.

Trigger-Ability-Motivation


How can this way of designing things, be used to promote behavioral changes among consumers to more socially and environmentally sound products and services?
